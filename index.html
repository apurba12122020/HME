Abstract

The study addresses the challenge of interobserver variability in treatment decisions for kidney cancers. We propose an end-to-end deep learning model leveraging multi-phase CT scans to differentiate between five primary histologic subtypes of kidney cancers. The results showed AUC scores of 0.97 and 0.99 for distinguishing between cancerous tumors and adenomas...
